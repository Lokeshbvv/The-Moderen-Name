About Us

The Modern Frame was established in 2005 to promote modern and contemporary art in India. Since its inception, it has hosted over 60 exhibitions and introduced numerous emerging artists to the global scene.

Our History

From humble beginnings in a single-room studio to a nationally recognized gallery, our journey has been one of passion, creativity, and vision.
